Pick a keyword, and then youâ€™ll be provided with a handful of stocks of similar industry domain from which you can choose for your portfolio or research! Our Word-Based-Firm-Search, WBFS, allows you to look up firms by the keyword of your interest.

WBFS delivers a unique service, in coupling with BTIC, that is rarely available in any other industry classification systems.
